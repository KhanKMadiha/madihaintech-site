# Conversation Design

## Purpose of the Chatbot
This chatbot acts as a portfolio assistant. It introduces who I am, explains how I work, and demonstrates how I design AI conversations using a support-engineering mindset.

---

## Entry Message (What the user sees first)

Hello! I’m an AI assistant built by Madiha.  
I can help you learn about:
1. My background  
2. How I work (support mindset)  
3. Projects I’ve worked on  

What would you like to explore?

---

## Conversation Categories

### 1. About Madiha
**User intent:** Learn about my background

**Key points to cover:**
- Senior Product Support Specialist
- 5+ years in SaaS support
- Interest in AI support and operations
- IBM AI Fundamentals learning path

**Follow-up options:**
- Tell me about your support experience  
- Why are you interested in AI?  
- What are you learning now?

---

### 2. How I Work (Support Mindset)
**User intent:** Understand how I approach problems

**Key points to cover:**
- Structured troubleshooting
- Clear communication
- Handling unclear or incomplete questions
- Testing and iteration

**Follow-up options:**
- How do you handle ambiguity?  
- How do you test AI systems?  
- How do you think about user experience?

---

### 3. Projects
**User intent:** Learn about my work

**Key points to cover:**
- IBM AI Fundamentals chatbot (no-code)
- This portfolio chatbot project
- Future AI-related projects

**Follow-up options:**
- What did you learn from building this?  
- What would you improve next?

---

## Fallback & Clarification Strategy

If a user asks something unclear or unrelated:
- Ask a clarifying question
- Offer main categories again
- Keep responses short and friendly

Example:
“I can help with my background, how I work, or my projects. Which would you like to explore?”
