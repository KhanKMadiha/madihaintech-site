Voice of the Customer · 2026

Support Ticket Analyser

Live demo GitHub

The documentation gap is a product signal. I built the tooling to prove it.

An AI-native VoC workflow: paste a ticket, score it against the knowledge base, surface documentation gaps, draft a response, and publish a KB article directly to Notion. Steps 1 to 3 run live in the browser; step 4 uses a Cloudflare Worker to proxy the Anthropic and Notion APIs with secrets off the client.
